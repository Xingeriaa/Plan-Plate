Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, ordering process, and more.

Products & Ingredients

Yes, all of our products are certified organic. We work directly with farmers and producers who follow strict organic farming practices. Each product comes with proper certification to ensure you're getting genuine organic ingredients.

We source our ingredients directly from organic farmers and producers around the world. We prioritize fair trade practices and sustainable farming methods. By cutting out middlemen, we're able to offer higher quality products at better prices while ensuring farmers receive fair compensation.

We provide detailed allergen information for each product on its individual product page. Many of our products naturally contain allergens such as nuts, seeds, or gluten. We process different ingredients in separate facilities to minimize cross-contamination, but we always recommend checking the specific allergen information for each product if you have allergies or sensitivities.

The shelf life varies by product. Dried herbs, spices, and grains typically have a shelf life of 12-24 months when stored properly. Oils, nuts, and seeds generally have a shorter shelf life of 6-12 months. Each product comes with a best-by date and storage instructions to help you maximize freshness. We recommend storing most products in airtight containers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Ordering & Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 day delivery. International shipping times vary by location, generally taking 7-14 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees, import taxes, or duties that may apply when receiving international shipments. These fees are not included in our shipping charges and will be collected by your local postal service.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unopened products in their original packaging. If you receive damaged or defective items, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receipt. For food safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened food products unless they're defective. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive and process your return.

There is no minimum order amount required to place an order. However, orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental US. We offer bulk discounts for larger orders, with 5% off orders over $100, 10% off orders over $250, and 15% off orders over $500.

Account & Payment

While you can check out as a guest, creating an account offers several benefits: you can track your orders, save your shipping information for faster checkout, view your order history, and receive personalized recommendations. Account creation is free and only takes a minute.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For business customers, we also offer purchase orders and bank transfers for larger orders. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Yes, we take security seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We are PCI DSS compliant and never store your full credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party payment processors with industry-standard security measures.
